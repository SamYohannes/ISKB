Bonn, Germany;
A student hub, birthplace of Beethoven, bursting with beer and blossoms.

What to do in Bonn:

Cherry Blossoms
Cherry Blossoms can be found in places other than Japan and they're just a beautiful here.
Muesums
Visit a multitude of museums. So many options they've coined the area 'Museum Mile'
The birth place of Beethoven
Go and visit Beethoven's house while in Bonn.
Even if you only have a passing interest in classical music you’ll know something about Ludwig van Beethoven.


Where to stay in Bonn:

Moten One Bonn-Beethoven
On a bustling corner 4 minutes by foot from the Beethoven House museum,
this laid-back hotel is a 5-minute walk from the Rhine River
Arkadenschlösschen Bonn
This property is also rated for the best value in Bonn!
Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
Ibis Hotel
Cheap, cheerful, well located and has everything you need. What more can you want? Ibis is always a safe and great option.

Where to eat in Bonn:

Aennchen
Clearly a Bonn hidden gem - brilliant food, great service and all served in the LindenGarten on a warm summer's evening.
Really worth a visit when you are looking for good food and winelist.
Brauhaus Bönnsch
The unfiltered ale is a must at this congenial brewpub adorned with photographs of famous politicians great and failed,
from Willy Brandt to, yes, Arnold Schwarzenegger.
Eislabor
Delicious food and appealing prices, in a great decor.
